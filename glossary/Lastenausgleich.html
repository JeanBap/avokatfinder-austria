Burden Equalization

A statutory system compensating war-displaced persons in Austria.

What Is Burden Equalization?

Austria's Lastenausgleich system was created post-WWII to equalize war burdens.

It involves levies on capital and property to fund compensation.

Affects property assessments and transaction values.

Key in understanding Austrian property tax history.

Still relevant in some property transactions and valuations.

Key Facts

  • Post-war compensation system
  • Statutory in Austrian law
  • Affects property valuations
  • Historical significance
  • Part of property documentation

Common Mistake

Not all properties are affected equally; assessment depends on acquisition date and history.

Expert Tip

When purchasing Austrian property, understand how Lastenausgleich might affect your legal status and taxes.

FAQs

Does it apply to all properties?

It depends on property history and acquisition date.

How does it affect modern transactions?

Usually minimal impact now, but historically significant properties may be affected.

Is it still collected?

Collection rates are now minimal or ceased in most cases.
