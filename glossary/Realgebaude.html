Real Building Right

The right to own a building separately from the underlying land in Austria.

What Is Real Building Right?

Gebaudeeigentum allows building ownership apart from land ownership.

Creates complex property relationships in Austrian real estate law.

Requires careful documentation and registration.

Affects financing and insurance significantly.

Must be clearly distinguished from land ownership in contracts.

Key Facts

  • Separate from land ownership
  • Must be registered
  • Creates dual titles
  • Affects financing terms
  • Requires careful legal documentation

Common Mistake

Many assume a building automatically includes the land; this is not true with Gebaudeeigentum.

Expert Tip

If purchasing an Austrian property with building rights only, ensure comprehensive insurance and financing agreements.

FAQs

Who owns the land then?

The land owner is a separate party; you have rights to build and use.

Can I buy the land later?

Possibly, depending on the original contract and land owner agreement.

How does this affect mortgages?

Banks require special documentation and may impose additional conditions.
