What is Grundbuchauszug (Land Register Extract)?

An official certified copy of the relevant entries from the Land Register for a specific property.

Key Facts — Grundbuchauszug (Land Register Extract)
  • Official certified copy of Land Register entries for a property
  • Shows owner name, property description, and all encumbrances
  • Issued by district courts maintaining the Land Register
  • Essential document for property sales and mortgage applications
  • Updated regularly as property interests change

The Grundbuchauszug is an official document issued by the Austrian courts that contains all relevant entries from the Land Register for a specific property. It shows the current owner, all mortgages and encumbrances, easements, and other rights affecting the property. This document is essential for property transactions and verification of ownership.

The Land Register (Grundbuch) is maintained by district courts in Austria and contains detailed information about all real property. The Grundbuchauszug provides a certified copy of the relevant register pages for a particular property. It serves as official evidence of ownership and any restrictions on the property.

The Grundbuchauszug includes information organized into different sections: the main sheet showing the property description and owner, and additional sheets listing all encumbrances and rights. This structured format makes it easy to understand the property's legal status. The document is regularly updated as changes occur.

In property transactions, buyers typically require a current Grundbuchauszug to verify the seller's ownership and identify any mortgages or other encumbrances that will be discharged. Lenders also require this document before approving mortgages. The extract provides the foundation for due diligence in real estate deals.

Obtaining a Grundbuchauszug from the relevant district court is a straightforward process that can be completed online or in person. The court issues certified copies within a few days. Property owners can request extracts at any time, and the information is public for anyone with a legitimate interest.

Common Mistake: Using an old Grundbuchauszug in property transactions. You should always obtain a current extract to ensure you have the latest information about ownership and encumbrances.
Expert Tip: Request a Grundbuchauszug early in the property purchase process. This document helps identify any issues with the property and allows time to address them before completing the transaction.

Frequently Asked Questions

How current is the Grundbuchauszug issued by the court?

The extract reflects all entries as of the date of issuance. Recent transactions may take a few days to appear.

Can I obtain a Grundbuchauszug for any property in Austria?

Yes, the Land Register is public and anyone can obtain an extract for any property.

How long is a Grundbuchauszug valid for property transactions?

While not officially "expired," it's best to use extracts less than a few months old for transactions.
