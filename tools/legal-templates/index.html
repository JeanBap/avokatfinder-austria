📋 NDA / Confidentiality Agreement

Bilingual
⚖️ Legal basis: UWG §26 (Geschäftsgeheimnisse); Bundesgesetz gegen den unlauteren Wettbewerb; ABGB §1295 (allg. Schadenersatz); implementiert EU-Richtlinie 2016/943 durch UWG-Novelle 2018
NON-DISCLOSURE / CONFIDENTIALITY AGREEMENT

Parties: [Disclosing Party — full name, registration number, address]
         [Receiving Party — full name, registration number, address]
Date: [DATE]   Jurisdiction: Austria

1. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
"Confidential Information" means all technical, commercial, financial and business information disclosed by the Disclosing Party, whether marked confidential or of a nature that indicates confidentiality.

2. OBLIGATIONS
The Receiving Party shall: (a) keep Confidential Information strictly secret; (b) not disclose to third parties without prior written consent; (c) use solely for the purpose of [PURPOSE]; (d) apply at least the same level of protection as it applies to its own confidential information (minimum reasonable care).

3. EXCLUSIONS
No obligations apply to information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of Receiving Party; (b) was known to Receiving Party before disclosure; (c) was independently developed; (d) was lawfully received from a third party; (e) must be disclosed by law or court order.

4. TERM: [N] years from the date of this Agreement.

5. REMEDIES
A breach may cause irreparable harm. The Disclosing Party is entitled to seek injunctive relief in addition to damages. Contractual penalty: [AMOUNT €] per breach.

6. GOVERNING LAW: Austria law. Disputes: [City] courts / arbitration.

Signatures: Disclosing Party _______________  Receiving Party _______________

📋 Engagement Letter / Mandate Agreement

Bilingual
⚖️ Legal basis: Rechtsanwaltsordnung (RAO) §9; Allgemeines Bürgerliches Gesetzbuch (ABGB) §§1002–1044 (Vollmacht); Disziplinarrecht der Rechtsanwaltskammer
ENGAGEMENT LETTER / MANDATE AGREEMENT

Date: [DATE]
Law Firm: [Name], [Bar registration number], [Address]
Client: [Name / Company], [Registration], [Address]

1. SCOPE OF MANDATE
The Law Firm is instructed to: [describe matter — e.g. advise on and represent Client in [MATTER] before [Court/Authority]]

2. FEES
Hourly rate: [€ AMOUNT] + applicable tax
OR Fixed fee: [€ AMOUNT] + applicable tax
Retainer: [€ AMOUNT] payable within [N] days of this letter.
Disbursements (court fees, translations, expert fees) are charged at cost in addition.

3. BILLING: Invoices issued [monthly / on completion]. Payment within 14 days.

4. PROFESSIONAL CONDUCT
The Law Firm is subject to the professional rules of [Bar Association]. All communications are subject to legal professional privilege/professional secrecy.

5. TERMINATION: Either party may terminate on [N] days' written notice. Outstanding fees are payable on termination.

6. GOVERNING LAW: Austria law.

Signed: Law Firm _________________  Client _________________

📋 Demand Letter / Letter Before Claim

Bilingual
⚖️ Legal basis: ABGB §1334 (Verzug); ABGB §1333 (Verzugszinsen); Zahlungsverzugsgesetz (ZVG 2013, implementing EU Directive 2011/7); gesetzlicher Zinssatz 4% p.a. (§1000 ABGB), Verzugszinsen B2B: 9.2% über EZB-Basiszinssatz
DEMAND LETTER / LETTER BEFORE CLAIM

From: [Creditor name, address]
To:   [Debtor name, address]
Date: [DATE]   Reference: [REF]

Dear Sir/Madam,

We act on behalf of [CLIENT] (the "Creditor") and write regarding the outstanding sum of:
[AMOUNT €] ([AMOUNT IN WORDS])

Arising from: Invoice No. [NR] dated [DATE] / Agreement dated [DATE]
Original due date: [DATE]

INTEREST
Statutory/contractual interest accruing at [RATE]% per annum from [DATE] to date of payment amounts to approximately [€ AMOUNT] to date.

DEMAND
We hereby formally demand payment of the total sum of [€ AMOUNT] (principal + interest) within 14 days of the date of this letter.

Payment should be made to: [BANK], IBAN: [IBAN], BIC: [BIC]

CONSEQUENCES OF NON-PAYMENT
If payment is not received within the specified period, our client reserves the right to commence legal proceedings without further notice. The costs of such proceedings, including legal representation costs, will be sought from you.

Yours faithfully,
[Name / Law Firm]  [Date]
