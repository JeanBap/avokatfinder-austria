How to Choose the Right Legal Professional in Austria

1. I need to buy a property in Vienna

Property transactions require specific expertise in real estate law, tax, and notarization.

→ Notar (Notary) + optional Immobilien-Rechtsanwalt
Notary is mandatory for property transfers. Attorney recommended for complex deals. Budget: €3,000-8,000 total.

2. I was wrongfully dismissed from my job

Employment disputes require knowledge of Angestelltengesetz, Arbeitsverfassungsgesetz, and case law.

→ Arbeitsrecht-Rechtsanwalt or Arbeiterkammer (AK)
Labor lawyer recommended. AK offers free advice to employees. Court fees ~2.5% of claim value.

3. I'm starting a GmbH / AG and need compliance advice

Corporate formation requires notarized documents, Firmenbuch registration, tax planning.

→ Notar + Steuerberater + Unternehmensrechtsanwalt
Notary for Gesellschaftsvertrag (mandatory). Tax advisor for KöSt/ESt planning. Startup costs: €500-2,000.

4. I have a business dispute over €85,000 unpaid invoice

Commercial litigation through Austrian courts (Bezirksgericht → Landesgericht). ZPO proceedings.

→ Wirtschaftsrechts-Rechtsanwalt
Specialized commercial litigator. Court fees: ~€2,125 (2.5% of €85k). Attorney: €200-500/hr × ~50 hrs = €10-25k.

5. I'm going through a divorce and need child support advice

Family law disputes involve Obsorge (custody), Unterhalt (maintenance), ABGB §§55-100.

→ Familienrechts-Rechtsanwalt or Mediator
Consensual divorce (§55a ABGB) can be resolved by mutual agreement. Mediation: €300-600/session. Contested: €5-15k.

6. I need to do tax planning for my freelance business

Tax optimization between Einzelunternehmer (ESt) and GmbH structures. USt, KöSt, social insurance analysis.

→ Steuerberater (Tax Advisor)
Essential for tax planning. Budget: €2,000-5,000/year depending on complexity.

7. I need to settle a dispute without going to court

Mediation, arbitration (VIAC), or ombudsman complaints faster and cheaper than court.

→ Mediator or VIAC Schiedsverfahren
Mediation: €500-2,000 one-time. Arbitration: €1,000+ but final decision. Court: €2,500+.

8. I'm being investigated for alleged fraud / white-collar crime

Criminal defense requires Strafrecht expertise, Beschuldigtenrechte (§164 StPO), court procedures.

→ Strafrecht-Rechtsanwalt (Criminal Lawyer)
Critical to engage immediately. Legal aid (Verfahrenshilfe, §61 StPO) available if income <€1,200/mo.

9. I need to collect a debt from a non-paying customer in another EU country

Cross-border debt recovery, enforcement procedures, Brussels I Recast regulation.

→ Internationales Privatrecht-Rechtsanwalt or Inkassobüro + Anwalt
Collection agency may attempt amicable settlement. Attorney needed for enforcement. Budget: €5-20k.

10. I need to register a trademark / patent in Austria and EU

Intellectual property registration with Österreichisches Patentamt (ÖPA) and EUIPO coordination.

→ Patentanwalt (IP Attorney)
Specialized IP counsel. Trademark AT: €360 + €180/class. Patent: €110 app + €200 exam. Attorney: €2-5k total.
